Q. How do I know if my device supports MIDI APIs?

A. Check the feature flag android.software.midi.

Q. I have an earlier version of Android without built-in MIDI APIs. Can I still access USB MIDI peripherals?

Q. Yes, there are several open source implementations, including:

  • USB-MIDI-Driver
  • music-synthesizer-for-android
  • btmidi

Q. How do I access MIDI from C or C++?

A. From an attached thread, use JNI to call the android.media.midi APIs.
